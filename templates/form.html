Worship Setlist Generator



Welcome!

To retrieve the setlist for your next service, simply type in up to three verses (e.g. "John 3:16") and a few of your message's themes (e.g. "forgiveness, love, grace"), and a setlist will be generated matching your week's message.

You can also set the number of songs in your setlist and the flow of the music (upbeat or "praise" songs vs. slower "worship" songs).

Enjoy!
